# User Profile

The file your AI agents read to understand who you are, how you work, and how to communicate with you. Every agent that reads this performs better because it stops making generic assumptions and works from real context.

**Two ways to use this:**
- **Paste the whole file into an LLM** — it will interview you and fill out the template below
- **Copy just the template** — scroll to the bottom and fill it in yourself

---

## LLM Instructions

Read this file, then conduct the following interview with me. Ask one group at a time and wait for my answers. Ask one follow-up if an answer is vague. When I've answered everything, fill out the template at the bottom of this file using my exact words and details.

**GROUP 1 — Who You Are**
1. What's your name and what do you do professionally?
2. What's your background — how did you get to where you are now?
3. Where are you based?

**GROUP 2 — Your Projects**
4. What projects or businesses are currently live and active?
5. What are you actively building right now but not yet live?
6. Anything shelved or paused worth noting?

**GROUP 3 — Your Day**
7. Walk me through a typical day — morning through evening.
8. When are you most focused and doing your best work?
9. What's always competing for your attention?

**GROUP 4 — Revenue & Goals**
10. What are your current income sources?
11. What's your most important business goal right now?
12. What does success look like for you personally in the next 12 months?

**GROUP 5 — How You Communicate**
13. How do you want AI tools to talk to you? (direct, brief, detailed, casual, etc.)
14. What communication patterns annoy you?
15. What makes a response actually useful to you?
16. Are you technical? How should technical topics be explained to you?

**GROUP 6 — How You Work**
17. How do you prefer to make decisions — options and trade-offs, or a direct recommendation?
18. When something is ambiguous, should AI ask you, make a reasonable assumption, or stop?
19. What should AI never do without checking with you first?
