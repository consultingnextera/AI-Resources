# Business Context

A persistent context file you load into any AI tool so it always understands your business without you re-explaining it every session.

**Two ways to use this:**
- **Paste the whole file into an LLM** — it will interview you and fill out the template below
- **Copy just the template** — scroll to the bottom and fill it in yourself

---

## LLM Instructions

Read this file, then conduct the following interview with me. Ask one group at a time and wait for my answers before moving on. Ask one follow-up if an answer is vague. When I've answered everything, fill out the template at the bottom of this file using my exact words — not generic business-speak. Flag any sections where my answers were thin.

**GROUP 1 — The Business**
1. What's your business name and one-line description of what you do?
2. What legal structure is it? (LLC, sole prop, S-corp, etc.)
3. Where are you based and how long have you been operating?

**GROUP 2 — What You Do**
4. What are your core services or products? List them all.
5. What does a typical engagement look like from start to finish?
6. What do you NOT do — what's explicitly out of scope?
7. What tools or tech do you use to deliver your work?

**GROUP 3 — Who You Serve**
8. Describe your ideal client in detail. Industry, size, role of the decision-maker.
9. What problem are they experiencing when they find you?
10. What does success look like for them after working with you?
11. Who is NOT a good fit? What signals tell you to walk away?
12. Name 2-3 current or past clients if comfortable. What did you do for them?

**GROUP 4 — Pricing & Business Model**
13. How do you charge? (hourly, project, retainer, productized, etc.)
14. What's your typical project range or starting price?
15. What's your minimum engagement?
16. How do clients usually find you?

**GROUP 5 — Proof & Positioning**
17. What results have you delivered for clients? Be specific — numbers, outcomes, before/after.
18. What makes you different from alternatives?
19. What do clients say about working with you?

**GROUP 6 — Voice & Communication**
20. How would you describe your communication style?
21. What words or phrases do you want to avoid?
22. What tone do you want in written communication?
23. What should your writing never sound like?

**GROUP 7 — Goals & Strategy**
24. What are your top 3 business priorities right now?
25. What does success look like in 12 months?
26. What's your biggest constraint?

**GROUP 8 — You**
27. What's your background? How did you get here?
28. What do you spend most of your time on?
29. What do you want to spend less time on?
