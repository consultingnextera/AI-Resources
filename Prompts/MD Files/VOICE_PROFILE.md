# Voice Profile

The file your AI content agents read before writing anything in your name. Without this, every piece of content sounds like the same generic AI. With it, your agents write in your specific tone, avoid your pet peeves, and follow your platform-specific rules.

**Two ways to use this:**
- **Paste the whole file into an LLM** — it will interview you and fill out the template below
- **Copy just the template** — scroll to the bottom and fill it in yourself

---

## LLM Instructions

Read this file, then conduct the following interview with me. Ask one group at a time and wait for my answers. Push back if an answer is too vague — specific examples are worth more than adjectives. When I've answered everything, fill out the template at the bottom of this file using my exact examples and language.

**GROUP 1 — Your Audience**
1. Who reads most of what you write? Be specific — not "business owners" but exactly who, what they care about, what would make them stop reading.
2. What do they want to read from you?
3. What tone or approach would make them dismiss you immediately?

**GROUP 2 — Your Voice**
4. How would you describe your writing personality in 3 words?
5. Who do you write like — any person, publication, or style that's close?
6. What's the single most important quality your writing must always have?
7. What does your writing absolutely never sound like?

**GROUP 3 — What You Never Say**
8. List 5-10 words or phrases you hate seeing in content.
9. What patterns do you always edit out when you review AI-generated content?
10. Give me a real example of writing that sounds nothing like you.

**GROUP 4 — Platform by Platform**
11. How does your tone change on LinkedIn vs email vs client documents?
12. For LinkedIn specifically: what's your hook style, your structure, your pet peeves?
13. For client-facing documents: what tone makes them feel right?

**GROUP 5 — Formatting**
14. Bullet points or prose? When does each apply?
15. How long should different things be? (post, email, proposal)
16. Anything about formatting you care about strongly?

**GROUP 6 — Examples**
17. Paste 1-2 pieces of your writing that sound exactly like you.
18. Paste 1 piece of AI-generated content that sounds nothing like you — and tell me what's wrong with it.
