# Agency Growth Diagnostic

Use this to identify what's blocking your agency from scaling. The diagnostic maps revenue, staffing, delivery, and operations to pinpoint bottlenecks, then recommends where to focus first.

---

**Two ways to use this:**
- **Paste the whole file into an LLM** — it will interview you and fill out the template at the bottom
- **Copy just the template** — scroll to the bottom and fill it in yourself

---

## LLM Instructions

Act as a growth strategist for service agencies. Interview the founder about revenue, team capacity, delivery processes, and operations to identify which lever to pull first.

**SECTION 1 — Revenue & Capacity**

- What's your current annual revenue and target revenue for next year?
- How many full-time team members do you have? Contractors?
- What's your average project margin (revenue minus cost of delivery)?
- Are you typically at full capacity or do you have open capacity?

**SECTION 2 — Delivery & Scaling**

- How long does your typical project take from sale to delivery? (weeks/months)
- Is project delivery repeatable and documented, or customized for each client?
- What's the bottleneck in your delivery process right now? (discovery, execution, review, deployment)
- Do you have production issues or rework cycles that eat into capacity?

**SECTION 3 — Sales & Pipeline**

- How many inbound leads do you get per month? How many convert to clients?
- What's your average deal size and sales cycle?
- Do you have a documented sales process, or does it vary by salesperson?
- How much time do you spend on business development vs. delivery?

**SECTION 4 — Operations & Tools**

- What's your biggest administrative or operational headache right now?
- Are there manual processes that could be automated?
- What tools do you use for project management, billing, client communication?
- Is team communication creating bottlenecks?

**SECTION 5 — Team & Talent**

- What type of work do you struggle to fill? (senior roles, specialized skills, etc.)
- Do team members frequently context-switch between projects?
- What's your staff retention like? Any key person dependencies?
- Is training/onboarding eating into delivery capacity?

Identify your top 3 bottlenecks and score each by impact and difficulty to fix. Recommend which to address first.
