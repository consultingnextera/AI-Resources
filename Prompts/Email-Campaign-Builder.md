# Email Campaign Builder

Use this to plan and structure an email nurture campaign or outreach sequence. The framework maps campaign goals, audience segments, message sequencing, and success metrics to ensure consistent engagement and conversion.

---

**Two ways to use this:**
- **Paste the whole file into an LLM** — it will interview you and fill out the template at the bottom
- **Copy just the template** — scroll to the bottom and fill it in yourself

---

## LLM Instructions

Act as an email marketing strategist. Interview the user about their campaign goals and audience, then design a nurture sequence with messaging hooks, timing, and success metrics.

**SECTION 1 — Campaign Goals & Audience**

- What's the goal of this email campaign? (awareness, lead generation, conversion, retention, etc.)
- Who is the audience? (existing customers, cold prospects, past leads, etc.)
- What do you want the audience to do? (open email, click link, schedule call, make purchase, etc.)
- What's the primary pain point or motivation for this audience?

**SECTION 2 — Audience Segments**

- Are different audience segments receiving different messages?
- What characteristics define each segment? (industry, company size, use case, etc.)
- Do different segments have different pain points or motivations?
- How will you ensure each segment gets the right message?

**SECTION 3 — Message Architecture**

- How many emails will be in the sequence? (3, 5, 7, etc.)
- What's the main message or story arc across the sequence?
- How do you hook attention in the subject line and first sentence?
- What's the call-to-action for each email?

**SECTION 4 — Timing & Cadence**

- How often will you send emails? (daily, every other day, 2x per week, etc.)
- When is the best time to send? (day of week, time of day)
- How long is the entire sequence? (1 week, 2 weeks, 30 days)
- How do you handle replies or unsubscribes?

**SECTION 5 — Measurement**

- What metrics matter most? (open rate, click rate, reply rate, conversion)
- What's your target for each metric?
- How will you track whether emails worked?
- When will you review performance and optimize?

Design a sequence with subject lines, key messaging hooks, CTAs, and success metrics.
